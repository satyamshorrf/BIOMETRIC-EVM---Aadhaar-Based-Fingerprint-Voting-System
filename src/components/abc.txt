## **Design / Style Guidelines**

* **Primary Colors:** Deep blue (#1E3A8A), Purple (#7C3AED), White (#F9FAFB)
* **Secondary Colors:** Soft gray (#F3F4F6), Gold (#F59E0B) for medal highlight
* **Typography:**

  * Headings: `Poppins` or `Inter`, bold
  * Body: `Roboto` or `Inter`, regular
  * Sizes:

    * H1: 4xl–6xl
    * H2: 3xl–4xl
    * Body: base–lg
* **Buttons:** Rounded-full, gradient background, shadow, hover scale-up
* **Animations (Framer Motion):**

  * Fade-in on scroll for sections
  * Hover scale-up on images/videos
  * Slide-in for hero text

---

### **Gallery / Media**

* Images: `object-cover`, `rounded-lg`, hover scale
* Videos: small autoplay muted clips or modal pop-up
* Lightbox for enlarged view

---

### **Other UI Tips**

* Use `container mx-auto px-4` for consistent spacing
* Section spacing: `py-16` for clear separation
* Shadow & rounded corners for cards (`shadow-lg rounded-xl`)
* Consistent iconography for steps/technology/achievements
